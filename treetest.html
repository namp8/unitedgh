About Us

UNITED Plastics & Packaging Industries Ltd. is into flexible & rigid plastic packing, and started its operations in 2003 after identified a market opportunity gaps for plastic shopping bags. It was the first company in Ghana to manufature Caps & Closures and Disposable Cutlery.

The company is run and operated by CEO Mr. Jagdish R. Jaisingh and has continued to grow substantially over the last 15 years. It began its operations operating initially from a 2400 square foot warehouse, with one single screw film extruder purchased from Argentina and three shopping bag cutting and sealing machines from South Korea. Within four years, the company manufactured 250 million bags every month. Now, in 2018, the company is producing over 600 million shopping bags and over 50 million closures monthly.

UNITED Plastics & Packaging Industries Ltd. produces shopping bags, FFS film on roll, disposable cutlery, caps and closures, containers for the food industry, household plastic products, etc. Some of the products are produced using a process known as injection molding and blown film extrusion which are all 100% biodegradable.

The key competitive advantages behind the company's growth is based on three important values:

Trusted Quality

UNITED Plastics has continued to sustain it's policy of "We do not compromise on quality." We believe that innovation is at the heart of our product quality.

Environmentally Friendly

We are very conscious of the impact to mother nature. UNITED Plastics produces 100% Biodegradable plastic bags and films, whose components are derived from renewable raw materials, ensuring that all products are environmentally friendly.

Timely Delivery

Our reliable workforce promises speed at all levels of the process. Therefore, all of our orders are delivered on time to our clients to sustain strong and long-term business relationships.
